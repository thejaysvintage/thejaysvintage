Hello there! We are The Jays, a three piece Midlands based vintage tribute vocal group who love to excite the ears with close harmonies, toe tapping rhythms and memory jogging tunes. We resurrect the wonderful hits of the 1940's, including those of the inimitable Andrews Sisters, Glenn Miller and Vera Lynn.

Our style suits Vintage themed events such as: Military and Historical, corporate work, fairs, weddings and much more!
If you can 1940's theme it - we can sing it!

What do we do?

ANDREWS SISTERS
STYLE

  • Beautiful Vintage Harmonies
  • Bop the night away to the wonderful sounds of those WW2 years

  • Andrew from the Jays also does 1950s/60s Rock n Roll as 'Andy B Goode'. We offer joint events if required.
